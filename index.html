Round Table After Dark

The Roundtable After Dark” is a chilling horror audio drama that follows a group of friends hosting a late-night podcast every Friday the 13th, exploring the supernatural. What starts as a fun, spooky-themed episode quickly turns sinister when they experiment with a mysterious Ouija board. As the night unfolds, strange occurrences and disturbing messages blur the line between entertainment and reality, with an unknown entity called “LOLA” taking control. Will they survive the night, or will their curiosity summon something far darker?
