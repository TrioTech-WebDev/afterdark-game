Round Table After Dark

The Roundtable After Dark is a horror audio drama that follows a group of friends who host a late-night podcast, on Friday the 13th, exploring the supernatural. What begins as a fun, spooky-themed episode quickly spirals into something far more sinister when they experiment with a mysterious Ouija board. As the night unfolds, the line between entertainment and reality begins to blur. Strange occurrences, disturbing messages, and an unknown entity known as “LOLA” begin to take over, turning their recording session into a fight for survival.

The Roundtable After Dark is meant to leave listeners questioning what’s real… and what followed them home.

How To Play

1. Start the Story
Play the introduction audio to understand the mystery and begin your investigation.

2. Explore Chapters
The game has 3 chapters. Each chapter contains 3 audio fragments to discover.

3. Arrange the Fragments
Listen carefully and place the fragments in the correct order to reconstruct each chapter’s events.

4. Unlock Full Chapters
Correct sequences unlock the complete chapter audio. Solve all 3 chapters to progress.

5. Solve the Final Mystery
Arrange the chapters in the correct order to unlock the final epilogue and reveal the truth.
